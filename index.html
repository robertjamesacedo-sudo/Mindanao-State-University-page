About MSU

Mindanao State University is one of the premier state universities in the Philippines, established to provide quality education and promote cultural and ethnic harmony in Mindanao. Founded in 1961, MSU has grown to include multiple campuses across Mindanao, offering a wide range of academic programs and research opportunities.
